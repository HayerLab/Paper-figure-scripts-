

preprocessing and single cell analysis: 

- pre processing: starts with ND2 files, and outputs tiffstacks, background correction, channel alignment, and bleach corrected segmented channel data for full FOV. 

- single_cell_processing: crops cells from Tiff Stacks, outputs bleach-corrected , segmented and background corrected data for individual cells. 

These two master scripts are essential and must be run to perform the majority of the code in other folders. Other generally named folders contain scripts and functions used throughout the paper, with notes on where they were used. 


figure specific scripts: specialized functions and scripts used in the paper to generate individual figures and data.
